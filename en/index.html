ABOUT US

Since the first days of mobilisation, we have been doing everything we can to help people evade conscription, leave the country legally or illegally, find asylum and much more. Our mission is to make sure that as few people as possible pull the trigger.

3144 PEOPLE HAVE ALREADY RECEIVED AID

"Go by the Forest' is a team of experienced staff and hundreds of volunteers. People write to us on Telegram, and then our consultants provide legal and psychological assistance, help to find asylum, transfer across borders, and financial aid.
